Value Canvas

Introduction

The value canvas consists of a variety of guidelines, such as different methods or tools that startups and small businesses can use to effectively deal with value management. This artifact represents the first iteration and is a product of the author's master's thesis research.

Main areas

The first layer of the canvas represents the primary areas that drive value creation. Specifically, it focuses on the customer and market forces that influence product strategy and execution. These forces apply pressure, guiding decisions and shaping the overall functioning of this area.

Functional areas

The functional area showcases various sections that include common methods and tools, all aimed at achieving a shared goal.

Guidelines

The last layer consist of the guidelines itself, which represent a variety of methods,tools or strategies that can be utilized to achieve the objectives of the functional areas.

Additional Information

It is recommended to first review the author's master's thesis to understand the thought process behind the creation of this canvas. However, please note that this canvas is not a definitive artifact. Its online presence aims to establish a feedback loop, allowing professionals to share the tools and techniques they use. This collaborative effort aims to further optimize and continually update the canvas.
